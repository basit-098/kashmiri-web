OUR HERITAGE

The Sacred Art of Kandur

In the heart of every Kashmiri neighborhood stands the Kandur — the traditional bakery where day begins with the aroma of fresh bread. For centuries, these master bakers have kept alive the flame of our culinary heritage.

At Kashmir Cravings, we partner with third-generation Kandurs who still use wood-fired tandoors and hand-kneading techniques that make every loaf unique. When you bite into our bread, you taste not just flour and fire, but history itself.
